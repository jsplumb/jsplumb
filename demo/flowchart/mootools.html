FLOWCHART

This is a demonstration of the Flowchart connectors.

Hover over connections to highlight them, click to delete.

Drag new connections from blue dots to green dots. You can also drag connections from green dots onto other green dots or back onto themselves.

By default, Flowchart connectors have square corners, but by setting the 'cornerRadius' parameter, as we have here, you can get rounded corners.

This demonstration uses jsPlumb 1.5.2, MooTools 1.3.2 and MooTools More 1.3.2.1.
