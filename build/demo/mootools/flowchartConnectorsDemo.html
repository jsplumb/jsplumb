This is a demonstration of Flowchart connectors

Hover over connections to highlight them, click to delete.

Drag new connections from blue dots to green dots. You can also drag connections from green dots onto other green dots or back onto themselves.

This demonstration uses jsPlumb 1.4.0, jQuery 1.8.1 and jQuery UI 1.8.23.
